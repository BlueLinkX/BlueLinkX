## About Me / 鑷繁绱逛粙

I am a Ph.D. student at Waseda University working at the intersection of reinforcement learning, large language models, and autonomous systems. My research focuses on the development and evaluation of intelligent agents, decision-making and planning in complex environments, and AI-driven approaches to software and system reliability.
